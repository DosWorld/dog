DOG DEVELPOMENT FAQ

Where can I get the latest source?

The latest source can be found at the source page. This page holds the latest distributed version of DOG. I might be working on a new update so you might want to check at least once per week. You can also mail and ask for the newest source.

Who is involved in developing DOG?

Well at the moment it's only me, but I hope others will join in soon. Mail me if you want to join.

How can i help?

Depending on your programming skill you can look at the to do page and implement one of the todos or you can try to debug the source. You can also test DOG and mail the results of your test runs..
